Love Story

Andrew McKinleyFrom our first date I knew Angelica was different. She wasn’t just pretty and sweet, but she had that special something. She is smart and she has style. Her ambition, her open-mindedness, her everything made her more than just a woman to me. We went out for Coldstone on our first date and I couldn’t get enough of her after that day. I called her all the time and wanted to spend all of my time with her. It wasn’t long before I wanted to make us official. As stayed together our relationship strengthen to the point where I knew I had to take that next step. I though for months about the most romantic way to profess my love. We took a trip to Italy together and I knew that was my chance.

Angelica RogersThe year was 2007 and I was at a restaurant in Hampton with two of my girlfriends. Andrew, a fellow Hampton U. student, walked over and invited us to go bowling with his friends. He was a horrible bowler with a great smile. While driving back to campus, he texted my friend for my number. But somehow he never got it (lol). About a month later, I ran into him at another restaurant but he was on a date. Thinking about how nice and handsome he was at the bowling alley, I decided to message him on this new website called Facebook. And he replied. Several dates (and years) later, Andrew and I took our first European trip to Italy. When he proposed at the Uffizi Gallery in Florence (in front of the Birth of Venus painting, no less), I couldn’t help but reflect back to that day in 2007. By the way, I still don’t know how he managed traveling around Italy without losing the ring or accidentally telling me. It was THE most romantic day of my life.



Ceremony & Reception

Cedar Hall 312 Broadway Rd.
Memphis, TN 38135

Click here for directions

Built in 1846, Cedar Hall is one of the oldest properties remaining in Bartlett, TN. Not only is it a great place for a wedding, but the house is a quaint bed & breakfast. The ceremony will be held outside at 6:00pm in the formal garden behind the house. Cocktail hour, in the english gardens, will immediately follow. Afterwards, the reception will be held in The Stables at Cedar Hall where there will be dinner, desserts, drinks and dancing! The venue has plenty of parking on site with attendants to direct you to where you need to go. Cocktail attire requested.

During the month of May, the weather in Memphis can be quite warm. On May 6, 2015, the high was 86 degrees and the low was 64 degrees, so please dress accordingly. Memphis has an amazing food scene with many flocking to the city during May for its annual barbeque festival.
